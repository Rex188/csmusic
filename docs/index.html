Music-Self

Your music taste is your identity. Watch it grow.

"It's like bringing up a kid — not building a report."
The idea

A private, introspective space where your listening becomes a living landscape. No dashboards. No stats. No pressure. Just you and your music-self, growing together.

Music is not just sounds. It's a reflection of how you perceive the world.

What this is

✓ IS

✓ A garden that grows with your music
✓ A private space to explore yourself
✓ Gentle awareness — quiet patterns noticed
✓ Optional notes attached to songs
✓ Social masks — share a facet, not the whole

✗ IS NOT

✗ A dashboard or stats page
✗ AI that explains your emotions
✗ Forced journaling or streaks
✗ A social network / feed
✗ Gamification or pop-ups
Inspired by

🎬 SOUL (Pixar) — Souls as abstract personalities that develop through experience. Your music-self is becoming, not finished.

🌸 n-gen art (Bloom) — Spotify data → flower visualization per artist. Proves the garden metaphor works.

🌍 Sonosphere — 3D world shaped by listening behavior, not just content. What you repeat, explore, curate.

🔮 Soundgaze — 3D point cloud of audio features. Similar songs = near each other.
