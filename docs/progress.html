🎯 The Core Idea

Music is how you perceive the world. Your lens, made visible.

The way you hear music is the way you see everything else. Show someone your music-self and you're showing them your perspective — not your taste, your lens. Consensus isn't agreeing on facts. It's recognizing a shared way of perceiving.

⚙️ Design Decisions
  • Music source: Netease Cloud Music (QR login via api-enhanced), local files supported
  • Identity model: One evolving self — "like bringing up a kid"
  • Deep analysis: Heavy ML under the hood so the landscape feels accurate — users never see charts unless they dig
  • Reflection: User recognizes themselves in the visual. Optional notes encouraged, not required.
  • Social: Share a facet of your music-self via social masks. Different masks for different people.
  • Platform: Web first, then iOS. Simple email/password.
  • Vibe: Apple-style minimalism, dark, interactive, scroll-reactive. No p5.js.
  • No-go: No ads, no force, no pop-ups, no gamification, no feed, no AI explaining emotions.
📚 Inspirations

🎬 SOUL (Pixar)

Souls as abstract personalities developing through experience. Your music-self is becoming, not finished.

🌸 n-gen art (Bloom)

Spotify → flower per artist/genre. Garden metaphor proven. Went viral.

🌍 Sonosphere

3D world from listening behavior (repetition, exploration, curation). Behavior shapes landscape.

🔮 Soundgaze

UMAP on audio features → 3D point cloud. Similar songs = close together.

✨ The gap we fill

None evolve over time. None incorporate user reflection. None feel like a relationship. That's where Music-Self sits.
