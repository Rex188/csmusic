📍 Current Status

CONCEPT  Direction decided. Moving toward prototype planning.

Music-Self — a private visual garden that grows with your music taste. Inspired by SOUL (Pixar), Bloom, Sonosphere. Full design doc in project/design-questions.md.

🎯 Core Concept

Private introspective space

Your music taste = your identity. A landscape, not a dashboard.

Evolves over time

Like bringing up a kid. Grows forever, changes with you.

No pressure

No streaks, no gamification, no pop-ups. You don't have to do anything.

Social masks

Share a facet of your music-self, not the whole thing. Like in real life.

📚 Inspirations

🎬 SOUL (Pixar)

Souls as abstract personalities developing through experience. Your music-self is becoming.

🌸 n-gen art (Bloom)

Spotify → flower per artist/ genre. Garden metaphor proven to work visually.

🌍 Sonosphere

3D world from listening behavior (repetition, exploration, curation). Behavior shapes landscape.

🔮 Soundgaze

UMAP on audio features → 3D point cloud. Similar songs = close together.

⚙️ Key Design Decisions
  • Music source: Streaming APIs (Spotify primary), offline files supported
  • Deep analysis: Heavy ML under the hood — users never see charts unless they dig
  • Reflection: Optional notes attached to songs, rewarded but not required
  • Identity model: One evolving self, like bringing up a kid
  • Platform: Web first, then iOS
  • Vibe: Apple-style minimalism, dark mode, interactive, scroll-reactive
  • No p5.js — user explicitly rejected it
